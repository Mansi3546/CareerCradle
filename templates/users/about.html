About-Us


Career Cradle is a website, where Job-seekers, Students, Interns can come to in order to start their career properly. Career Cradle can be used to gain guidance from industry professionals and stay in contact with them. Career Cradle has only purpose that is to give our society well-trained professionals and give the qualified professionals the job they truly deserve. When a person has a job they like, it acts as an emotional, social and financial support in their live and Career Cradle seeks to help in the betterment of the society by providing people with stability.
